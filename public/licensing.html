About

Music licensing doesn’t have to be hard. Our dedicated team of music licensing professionals can offer personalized support every step of the way. From finding the perfect song to executing the license, you’ll deal with a single representative to ensure that your project receives the unique attention it deserves. To further facilitate the licensing process, Monstercat Connect was developed for our clients to access our catalog and find that perfect song.

What is Monstercat Connect?

Monstercat Connect is our proprietary music management software that allows you to discover, stream and download Monstercat music. The process of licensing is fast and easy since users can have any track downloaded while clearance is pending. A quick browse through Connect reveals a robust collection of music bolstered by three new track releases every week at the moment of distribution.
